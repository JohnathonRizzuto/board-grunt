Care for your piece

Treat it right,
keep it for life

  • Pre-treated with a food-safe blend of tung oil, coconut oil & beeswax.
  • Wash by hand with soap and water, then towel dry — never soak it.
  • Never put your board in the dishwasher.
  • Re-treat now and then with Board Grunt “Beauty Wax” or any cutting-board finish.
